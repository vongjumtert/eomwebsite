About Empire of the Mekong

Empire of the Mekong is a grand strategy game set in 500 BC, where players control various nations across Southeast Asia. Developed by Chhun Mengchhy, the game blends diplomacy, economy, and military tactics as players vie for control of the Mekong region. Manage trade, population growth, and diplomacy while expanding your empire through land and sea routes.

Our Vision

We aim to create a historically immersive strategy game that challenges players to think critically about leadership, diplomacy, and war. Our vision is to bring the ancient civilizations of Southeast Asia to life, giving players a rich experience of statecraft, cultural diversity, and military strategy. As we grow, we plan to expand our game development efforts and explore new frontiers in strategy gaming.

Game Features

  • Dynamic Trade Routes: Manage land and sea trade routes across the Mekong region.
  • Cultural Management: Balance your empire’s ethnic diversity and avoid rebellion.
  • Diplomacy & Warfare: Engage in diplomacy or declare war to gain control over key provinces.
  • Realistic Economy: Tax provinces, control resources, and manage trade agreements to boost your economy.
  • Naval Power: Command the seas to control naval trade routes and dominate rival empires.
  • Detailed Map: The game features a richly detailed map with 177 provinces and planned expansions.
